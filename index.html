How it holds together

One program, not four plans

Orchestration

Channels play as one

Every message has a home channel: SMS breaks it, push echoes it in-app, email tells the full story, the website carries it forward. Shared frequency care — with email and SMS unified in Iterable — means fans never get the same alert twice, and every audience knows exactly what it signed up for.

Data foundation

One fan, one profile

Every touchpoint — a Fan Fest opt-in, a Digioh form, a Ticketmaster deposit, an app download — resolves to a single profile. Our data team manages this through Microsoft Dynamics CRM and the Hightouch CDP; marketing builds on that foundation.

Measurement

Learn fast, apply forward

This is the launch of a brand that has never existed, so we move aggressively and let early signal shape later campaigns — sources that produce lasting fans, stories that convert, features members actually use. Hard benchmarks come once we have a baseline worth holding ourselves to.
